Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Discount Intelligence (the "App") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you install or use the App in connection with your Shopify-supported store.

1. Data controller

The data controller for the data processed in connection with the App is the operator of the App. You can contact us at the email address below. We process data necessary to provide the discount analytics service.

2. Personal information the App collects

When you install the App, we automatically receive certain information from your Shopify account via API, within the permissions you grant:

  • Shop information: Shop name, email, domain, currency, and settings (e.g. default margin, last sync time).
  • Discount data: Discount codes, price rules, and their usage statistics.
  • Orders and products: Information about orders, products, prices, costs, and discounts to provide analytics and recommendations.
  • Technical data: Session identifiers and tokens required for authentication and operation of the App within the Shopify admin (e.g. OAuth).

3. Purpose and legal basis

We use this information to provide the Service (discount profitability analysis, recommendations, sync with Shopify). The legal basis is performance of the contract (providing the App) and, where applicable, our legitimate interest in ensuring security and proper operation of the App.

4. Data retention and sharing

We retain your data for as long as your store uses the App. If you uninstall the App, we will delete your data within 48 hours, unless we are required to retain it by law. We do not sell your personal information. Data may be shared with technical service providers (e.g. hosting, database) necessary to operate the App; they act as processors under our instructions. If hosting or processing takes place outside the EEA, we use appropriate safeguards (e.g. standard contractual clauses) where required.

5. Cookies and similar technology

The App runs within the Shopify admin and may use session or cookie mechanisms necessary for authentication and operation (e.g. OAuth). We do not use cookies for advertising or cross-site tracking.

6. Your rights (GDPR / UK GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area or the UK, you have the right to: access your data, rectification, erasure (“right to be forgotten”), restriction of processing, data portability, and to object to processing. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (e.g. in your country of residence). To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.
